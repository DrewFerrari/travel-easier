Essential Travel Tips

Check Passport Validity

Ensure your passport is valid for at least 6 months beyond your travel date.

Pack Smart

Check airline baggage allowances and pack essentials in your carry-on.

Currency Exchange

Exchange some currency before departure and inform your bank about travel plans.
