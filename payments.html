Frequently Asked Questions

Are my payments secure?

Yes, all payments are processed through secure, encrypted channels. We use industry-standard SSL encryption and are PCI DSS compliant to ensure your financial information is protected.

How long does payment processing take?

Mobile money and card payments are processed instantly. Bank transfers may take 1-2 business days to reflect. International transfers typically take 3-5 business days.

Can I pay in installments?

Yes, we offer flexible installment plans for services above $500. You can choose 3-month or 6-month payment plans with zero interest. Contact our team to set up an installment plan.

What if my payment fails?

If your payment fails, please check your payment details and try again. If the issue persists, contact our support team immediately. We'll help you complete the payment using an alternative method.
