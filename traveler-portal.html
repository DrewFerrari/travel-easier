Required Documents for Visa Applications:

  • Valid Passport: Clear copy of bio-data page (6 months validity)
  • Passport Photos: 2 recent white background photos (35x45mm)
  • ID Document: National ID or residence permit
  • Proof of Funds: Bank statements (last 3 months)
  • Travel Itinerary: Flight bookings and hotel reservations
  • Employment Letter: Company letter with salary details
  • Marriage Certificate: If traveling with spouse
  • Birth Certificates: For minors traveling
